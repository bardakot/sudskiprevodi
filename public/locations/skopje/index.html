Court Translation in Skopje

Skopje is the administrative and legal centre of North Macedonia, home to the country's main courts, ministries, notarial offices, universities, and foreign embassies. When you need a certified translation that will be accepted by any of these institutions, the translator must hold an official licence issued by the Ministry of Justice — not just claim professional experience.

Sudski Prevodi MK is operated by Dimitar Bardakoski, a licensed court translator based in the Aerodrom municipality of Skopje. With over 3,000 pages translated and 50 five-star Google reviews, the service is trusted by lawyers, notaries, international recruitment agencies, and private clients across the city.

Whether you are near the City Park, in Čair, Karpoš, Kisela Voda, or anywhere else in Skopje, documents can be sent online and the completed certified translation will be delivered to your door at no extra cost.

# Sudski Prevodi MK

Official court-certified translations from Skopje, North Macedonia. Licensed translator. Same-day turnaround. Free delivery.

## About

Sudski Prevodi MK is an official court translation service based in Skopje, North Macedonia. Founded and operated by Dimitar Bardakoski, a licensed Court Translator certified by the Ministry of Justice of North Macedonia. The service specializes in certified translations of legal, academic, business, and general documents between English, Macedonian, Serbian, and Turkish. Over 3,000 pages translated across 4 years in operation.
